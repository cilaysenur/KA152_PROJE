Our Vision & Roadmap

Understanding why we embarked on this journey and our core objectives to transform the aviation experience.

Why "Aviation for Everyone"?

Air travel should be a bridge between cultures, yet for many, it remains a daunting challenge. In today’s aviation landscape, young people—especially those from rural backgrounds, facing socioeconomic barriers, or living with disabilities—often encounter significant accessibility hurdles.

From complex airport procedures to lack of inclusive physical infrastructure, the 'stress' of flying often outweighs the excitement of the journey.

We launched KA152 - Aviation for Everyone to dismantle these barriers. We believe that the sky belongs to everyone, and no young explorer should be grounded by lack of information or inaccessible environments.

Our Core Objectives

Our mission is built on three main pillars to transform the passenger experience:

1
PILLAR 1

Demystifying the Airport

Simplifying complex airport processes through educational content and guides tailored for first-time flyers.

2
PILLAR 2

Fostering Inclusivity

Designing a stress-free travel model that addresses the specific needs of individuals with disabilities.

3
PILLAR 3

Bridging the Gap

Reaching out to youth in rural and low-income areas to provide them with the tools and confidence to enter the world of aviation.

Target Groups

"Aviation for Everyone" aims to reach young individuals aged 17-25 who feel excluded from the world of flight due to various challenges.

Youth from Rural Areas

Many young people living in rural or geographically isolated regions lack exposure to international travel and airport environments. We aim to bridge this gap by providing them with the necessary knowledge and confidence to navigate the skies.

Individuals with Disabilities

Accessibility is a right, not a privilege. We focus on the unique needs of young passengers with physical or sensory disabilities, working towards a "stress-free" airport model that ensures dignity and ease of travel for everyone.

First-Time Flyers

The complexity and cost of aviation can be intimidating for those from low-income backgrounds. We support first-time flyers by demystifying airport procedures and showing that the world of aviation is open to them.
